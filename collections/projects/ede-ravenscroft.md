Complete redesign and redevelopment of the online presence for Ede & Ravenscroft, one of Britain's oldest tailors and robe makers. The project involved migrating from a legacy system to TYPO3 CMS, rebuilding the e-commerce catalogue, and delivering a responsive frontend aligned with the brand's heritage aesthetic.
